About Me

My name is Gemma and welcome to my space.

The world can seem like a dark and dreary place sometimes, especially when all we seem to see in the media is destruction and sadness. I wanted to create a place to solely promoted the pretty, sparkly and happy things in life…no matter how small! So I created Gemma’s Space, a place that I could share my love of pink and glitter and all the things that make the world a prettier place. I will be sharing a mix of lifestyle, fashion, beauty and homey blog posts that will hopefully entertain you as much to read them as it will for me to write them. Thank you for reading so far and I hope you stick around for the content. For now I will say be happy and keep smiling.
